Wanna Escape New York...

Santiago

Santiago is in the middle of the Island. Santiago de los Caballeros named after the 30 Spanish aristocrats who followed Columbus and settled here in 1495 was the country's first capital before it suffered an earthquake in 1562.

Santo Domingo

Santo Domingo is the capital of the Dominican Republic. It is located on the South side of the Island. There are many historial sites to explore which date back to the 1400s.

Punta Cana

Punta Cana is one of the biggest tourist attractions of the island. They welcome visitors from all over the world. Punta Cana, the easternmost tip of the Dominican Republic, abuts the Caribbean Sea and the Atlantic Ocean.
